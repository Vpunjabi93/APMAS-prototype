## SECTION 10 — FAQ / OBJECTION HANDLING

**Do I have time for this alongside a full-time job?**
It's built for one: 3 hours a week, Saturday evenings, live online, over 12 months. Steady and additive — not a sprint that wrecks your quarter.

**Will an online certificate actually carry weight?**
This is a live, cohort-based IIM Kozhikode programme with faculty-led sessions, a 2-day campus immersion and a graded Capstone — not passive recorded content. The credential is from a top-3 (NIRF 2025) institute.

**I'm in sales, not marketing. Is this too marketing-heavy?**
No. Dedicated modules cover sales analytics, lead scoring, revenue enablement and go-to-market. Integrating sales with marketing and analytics is the whole point.

**Another IIM has a cheaper AI-marketing course. Why this one?**
Those typically teach you to *think strategically* about AI in 4–6 months. This is 12 months and 120 hours of actually *building, deploying and automating* a growth engine — and it includes sales and GTM, not just marketing. Different depth, different outcome.

**Will the tools be outdated by the time I finish?**
You learn tool-agnostic frameworks (5A, full-funnel, GTM) taught through a current, faculty-updated stack — so you learn to *choose and route* tools, not memorise one that changes next quarter.

**Am I eligible?**
You need a graduate degree plus either 2 years in a marketing/sales function or 5 years in a managerial role. Not sure how your profile fits? Ask a counsellor when you enquire.
